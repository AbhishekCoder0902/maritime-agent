### Build Note
**Tool Choice & Reasoning:** 
I chose a Python script paired with the **Gemini 2.5 Flash API** and **GitHub Actions** because this stack is entirely free, infinitely scalable, and requires zero maintenance. Python's `feedparser` handles RSS scraping seamlessly. I chose Gemini because it offers a highly capable free tier with a massive context window, perfect for digesting dozens of full news articles at once. Make.com or Zapier could work, but they often hit paywalls or operation limits when processing large amounts of text or iterating through arrays of news data.

**Failure Handling:** 
- **Source Goes Down:** The `agent.py` script wraps the RSS fetcher in a `try/except` block for each URL. If gCaptain goes down, it simply skips it, logs the error, and relies on the other feeds (Splash247, Google News).
- **API Timeouts/Garbage LLM Output:** I used structured JSON generation (`response_mime_type="application/json"`). If Gemini hallucinates or returns malformed JSON, the script catches the `JSONDecodeError`, logs the raw text for debugging, and aborts before sending a broken email.
